Shipping Info FREE SHIPPING TO STORE

You may also select the option to pick your item up in any of our store locations. This is a free service and the order can be
picked up during our normal store business hours (click here to see hours for your store location.) You will receive an email
notifying you when your order is ready for pickup. You have 15 days from the time you are emailed that your order is ready for
pickup to return (for store credit) or exchange these items. After this time period, the order becomes final sale.

NOTE: Ship to Store orders are processed by our warehouse and then shipped to each store location. Please allow up to 6
business days for your store pickup order to arrive at its destination.

Please be sure to check your address thoroughly before placing your order, as it will be your financial responsibility to cover the
re-shipment of a package needing an address correction or update.
