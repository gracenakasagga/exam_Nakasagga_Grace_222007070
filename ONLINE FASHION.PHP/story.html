M.O.T is a Lagos-based ready-to-wear fashion brand that creates affordable, functional and fashionable pieces for women around the world. Launched in 2019 by Mo Agusto, our brand ethos revolves around our passion for our customers and placing the modern millennial woman at the center of everything we do.

Vestique launched as an online boutique in 2010 by these two college besties out of a spare bedroom in Morgan’s house. Countless trunk shows at local universities,
late nights packaging orders (along with early mornings at their full time jobs) and straight up sweat equity turned the little web shop into a clothier with 12 brick
and mortar locations throughout the Southeast and an e-com site that remains the heartbeat of the company. Now with a
team of over 100 full and part time girl bosses, the vestique fam works hard to bring you a unique and fun shopping experience. Get to know us personally on social
media! Follow us on instagram (@vestique) for a behind the scenes look at our day to day, outfit inspo and more—including but not limited to: our babies, vestique pups and travel! We love
to hear from you! Emails, DMs, chats and tags of you wearing our clothes truly make our day. Thank you for being a part of our story.

xoxo, Morgan & Caroline
